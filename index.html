History

All begun from the Grandfather Andreas Skaris, who was one of the best marble technicians in Drama and Kavala. With his brothers established a very big Company (Marmara Skari) that even nowdays its name is popular and well-known.They had 6 Quarryies in various locations of northen Greece. This company is selling various kinds of marble stone including a marble brand with the name "Nestos Marble"
